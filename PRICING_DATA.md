# Pricing Data

The recommendation engine currently uses manually curated pricing assumptions for major AI tools.

## Included Tools
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Cursor

## Example Assumptions
- Claude Max is often unnecessary for small teams
- Cursor Business becomes cost-effective only at scale
- Enterprise plans may not be justified for lightweight usage
